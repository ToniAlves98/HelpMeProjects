History of the site

HELP PME PROJECTS is a platform developed within the scope of a dissertation of the Master’s in Engineering Project Management at the University of Minho. The development team is a group of students who are part of the Integrated Master in Information System Management at the University of Minho.

Values

Excellence – we look to share knowledge in a simpler and more innovative way. Trust – the most experienced project managers are the engine of the platform. Ambition – we look to improve the success of Portuguese SMEs.

Mission and Vision

HELP PME PROJECTS mission's is to support Portuguese SMEs and to generate a positive impact on society, contributing to the adoption and improvement of Project Management practices by Portuguese SMEs. The HELP PME PROJECTS vision is to become a reference platform in Portugal in the area of Project Management. The HELP PME PROJECTS purpose is to contribute to the improvement of project management practices, by supporting SME project managers, students, and other potential users.
